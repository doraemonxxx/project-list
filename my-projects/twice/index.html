FANCY YOU

2019-04-22

TWICE Announces The 7th Mini Album and its Title Song ‘FANCY’! Falling in love to ‘NEW TWICE!’
- Title Song ‘FANCY’, captivating mellow mood POP Dance song “I FANCY YOU!” Suggesting ‘NEW TWICE’!
- New album ‘FANCY YOU’, completed various sounds with the participation of famous overseas composers ‘Charli XCX’, ‘MNEK’ and so on!
- Presents total 6 Tracks including 4 songs written by JIHYO-MOMO-SANA-CHAEYOUNG! ‘The Album written by TWICE sensibility’

TWICE announces The 7th Mini Album ‘FANCY YOU’.
‘Asia TOP1 Girl Group’ TWICE has returned. TWICE delivered happiness with bright, catchy songs and performance full of energy, gaining unlimited love of the public. This time, TWICE presents new, ample album with splendid and fascinating concept.
The new aspect of TWICE that surprises all, and much wider and deeper musical depth with members participating in lyrics, clearly shows their effort and growth.
The Title Song ‘FANCY’, a mellow mood POP Dance song that announces the musical change of TWICE, delivers totally different concept of color from what TWICE has shown until now.
Under the cloud of tropical light and strong sun, TWICE who departs to search for love, shouts the confession of love “I will never let you go” without hesitation.
Lyrics that contains provocation such as “Whoever likes first doesn’t matter” is totally different from the past, enough to multiple TWICE’s charm.
